Billing, Upgrades & Cancellation

📄 Invoicing

After you email us your package choice, we send you a formal invoice via email. The invoice includes all costs, VAT (if applicable) and payment instructions. Payment is required before installation starts.

📅 Monthly cancellation

All plans are monthly cancellable (30 days notice). If you cancel, the server will be decommissioned at the end of the billing period.

📈 Upgrades

You can upgrade your server at any time. The extra cost is calculated pro‑rata (ratio) for the remaining days of the current month.

Pro‑rata calculation: (new price – old price) ÷ 30 × remaining days = extra cost for that month.

Downgrades are also possible — please contact us to discuss the terms.
